About Us

IWING is a technology support in electrification,automation and agriculture, enabling a more sustainable and resource-efficient future. The company solutions connect with contractors,engineers,electricians and give right time services.

What we do

Team Electrical

We provide electrical team connecting platform,for getting new opportunity to part time full time and contract base work.

Electrical work

Home Automation

Automatic control of electronic devices in your home. These devices are connected to the Internet, which allows them to be controlled remotely With home automation.
